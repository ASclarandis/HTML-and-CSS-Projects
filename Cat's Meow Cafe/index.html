Meet the Kitties

Meet Pickles!

Pickles is a high energy and mischievous 8 month old kitten. He loves belly rubs and playing with feather toys.

Meet Remy!

Remy is the sweetest 4 month old kitten who loves to cuddle. She spends most of her day napping and making biscuits on a soft blanket.

Meet George!

George is a handsome, 9 year old senior cat. His favorite things are chin scratches, chicken treats, and a warm lap to curl up in.

Meet Lily!

Lily is an adorable 1 year old kitten who is an absolute angel. She loves to play with other cats and requires snuggles at bed time.

Meet Maxwell!

Maxwell is a distinguished, 12 year old gentleman. He demands cuddles and butt scratches at all times of the day.

Meet Luna!

Luna is a sweet and sassy 2 year old cat. She can be a diva, but she will always make up for it by giving you kisses and lap biscuits.

Meet Mochi!

Mochi is a silly 1 year old who just wants to play all day. His favorite toy is a laser pointer and he loves to chase the other cats around the cafe.

Meet Willow!

Willow is a shy 4 year old who is still coming out of her shell. She spends most of her day hanging out at the top of the cat tree, observing the other cats.

Meet Pumpkin!

Pumpkin is a calm and cuddly 6 year old kitty. He will do just about anything for a can of tuna.
